Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2015, ShikshaSathi was born out of a simple yet powerful idea - to bridge the gap between quality education and accessibility in India. We recognized that while India has some of the brightest minds and most dedicated educators, many students still struggle to find the right guidance and resources to reach their full potential.

Our mission is to democratize education by connecting students with the perfect educators, regardless of geographical barriers. We envision a future where every student in India has access to personalized, high-quality education tailored to their unique learning needs and aspirations.

What began as a small startup in New Delhi has now grown into a national platform serving over 15,000 students across 50+ cities. Our success stems from our unwavering commitment to educational excellence, innovative technology, and most importantly, the success of our students.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at ShikshaSathi

Educational Excellence

We maintain the highest standards of teaching quality, ensuring our educators are not just qualified but truly exceptional at helping students learn and grow.

Student-Centric Approach

Every decision we make prioritizes student success. From personalized learning plans to flexible scheduling, we adapt to student needs, not the other way around.

We constantly explore and implement new teaching methodologies and technologies to make learning more effective, engaging, and accessible.

Our Journey

Key milestones in ShikshaSathi's growth and impact

2015

Foundation

ShikshaSathi was founded by Dr. Priya Sharma with a small team of 5 educators in New Delhi, starting with just 50 students in our first year.

2017

First Expansion

Expanded to 5 cities across North India and crossed 1,000 active students. Launched our first digital learning platform.

2019

National Recognition

Featured in "Education Today" as one of India's most innovative education startups. Reached 5,000 students across 15 cities.

2020

Pandemic Response

Rapidly adapted our platform for fully online learning during COVID-19, providing free resources to 10,000+ students affected by school closures.

2022

AI Integration

Launched our AI-powered matching system that connects students with ideal tutors based on learning style analysis and performance data.

2023

Current Reach

Now serving 15,000+ students across 50+ cities with a network of 2,500+ expert educators and a 97% student satisfaction rate.
