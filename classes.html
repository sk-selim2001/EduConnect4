Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our classes and programs

How do I choose the right class for my child?

We offer free assessment tests to evaluate your child's current level and learning needs. Based on the results, our academic counselors will recommend the most suitable classes and tutors. You can also schedule a free demo class to experience our teaching methodology before enrolling.

What is the duration of each class?

Our standard class duration is 60 minutes. However, for competitive exam preparation and advanced courses, we recommend 90-minute sessions. The duration can be customized based on the student's attention span and learning requirements.

Can we change the tutor if not satisfied?

Absolutely. We offer a tutor replacement guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your tutor after the first 2 classes, you can request a change at no additional cost. We'll match you with another qualified tutor better suited to your learning style.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, all our programs include digital study materials curated by subject experts. For certain competitive exam preparations, we also provide physical books and practice papers. The study materials are regularly updated to match the latest syllabus and exam patterns.

What are the payment options available?

We accept all major payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and EMI options. You can pay monthly or opt for discounted quarterly/annual payment plans.
