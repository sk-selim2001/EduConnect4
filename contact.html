Common Contact Questions

What's the best way to contact ShikshaSathi?

For quick responses, we recommend using our WhatsApp number (+91 98765 43210) during business hours. For detailed inquiries, please fill out our contact form or email us at info@shikshasathi.com. Our team typically responds within 24 hours.

Do you offer home tutoring services?

Yes, we provide home tutoring services in all major cities across India. Please contact our support team with your location and requirements, and we'll match you with qualified tutors in your area. Home tutoring availability depends on tutor locations and subject requirements.

How can I become a tutor with ShikshaSathi?

We're always looking for passionate educators! Visit our "Join as Educator" page or contact our HR department at careers@shikshasathi.com. You'll need to submit your qualifications, go through our verification process, and complete a teaching demonstration if selected.

What are your holiday schedules?

Our centers follow local school holiday schedules with some exceptions. We remain open on most public holidays but may have reduced hours. Specific holiday schedules are posted at each center and on our website announcements section 2 weeks in advance.
